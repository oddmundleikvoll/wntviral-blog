## 1. Visual Workflow Builder (Game Changer)

**Zapier:** Linear, step-by-step automation. A → B → C. Simple, but limiting.

**Make.com:** Visual drag-and-drop canvas. You can see your entire workflow, branch logic, handle errors, and create complex automations that would be impossible in Zapier.

**Real example:** We built a lead nurturing system that:

- Enrolls others in different email sequences based on behavior
- Sends alerts if someone visits pricing twice
- Updates our CRM, Slack, and dashboard—all in one workflow

In Zapier, this required 5 separate "Zaps" and cost a fortune in tasks. In Make.com, it's one elegant workflow.

3. **Smaller community:** Harder to find random tutorials on YouTube (though growing fast).
